Data Retention

05

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy — or as required by law to resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

  • Shop and merchant data is retained while the app remains installed on your store.
  • Upon uninstallation, shop data is scheduled for permanent deletion within 48 hours.
  • Customer data associated with your store is permanently erased from our database upon a valid redaction request.
  • Usage and analytics data is retained in anonymized form for a shorter period unless required for security or legal compliance.

Shopify Compliance Webhooks

10

As required by Shopify, our app subscribes to and responds to mandatory compliance webhooks. These ensure merchant and customer data rights are honored at the platform level:

customers/data_request

When a customer requests their data from a store owner, we provide all relevant customer data to the merchant directly.

customers/redact

When a store owner requests deletion of a customer's data on their behalf, we permanently erase that data from our systems.

shop/redact

48 hours after a merchant uninstalls the app, all shop data is permanently deleted from our database.
